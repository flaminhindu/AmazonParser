The HAC-504AW filters are very well made and do an excellent job in all our humidifiers. The same filters are available in box stores but it's so easy to just Amazon the order. The first Honeywell unit we bought was a tower model that has been running well for years and when we needed another for a new location; we stuck with what was working and paid attention to the filter designation so we would not have to maintain two sizes. The HAC-504AWs are better because there is less to fool with and they come in a festive box that stacks well with others.
