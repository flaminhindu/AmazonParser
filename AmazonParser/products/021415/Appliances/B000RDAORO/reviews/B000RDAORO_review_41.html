3 of 3 people found the following review helpful
1 Poor substitute for original filter wick
ByWBB3 on April 23, 2011
Item Package Quantity: 1 Verified Purchase
I have a Honeywell 300 series upright humidifier.
As noted in other reviews, the physical specifications of this filter wick has been changed. It appears to be the correct height and diameter; HOWEVER, the thickness of this filter has been cut by about 50% and yet the price remains the same. This effectively cuts the surface area of the filter in half and seriously impairs the rate of evaporation. As a result, the efficiency of the humidifier is compromised. Living in a hard water area of the Southwest, the decreased surface area will also decrease it's useful life span by at least 50% or more as well. This has been a good humidifier for many years but I will send it to the landfill before I will continue to pay double or triple the support cost to run it. It will be cheaper in the long run to just replace it with a machine with a more readily available filter/wick.
